Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our microphone testing tool, privacy, compatibility, and troubleshooting.

Our Microphone Test Tool is a web-based application that allows you to test your microphone's functionality directly in your browser. It provides real-time audio visualization, technical specifications, and detailed reports about your microphone's performance without requiring any downloads or installations.

The test uses your browser's Web Audio API to access your microphone and analyze the audio input in real-time. Here's how it works:

  • Requests microphone permission from your browser
  • Captures audio input and analyzes volume levels
  • Displays real-time visualization of audio frequencies
  • Collects technical specifications (sample rate, bit depth, etc.)
  • Generates a comprehensive report with all findings

No, you don't need to install any software! Our tool runs entirely in your web browser using modern web technologies. Simply visit our website, allow microphone access when prompted, and start testing immediately.

Absolutely not!

Your audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript. The audio data is analyzed in real-time for visualization and immediately discarded. We have zero access to your microphone input.

Browser permission requests are a security feature designed to protect your privacy. When you click "Start Test," your browser asks for explicit consent to access your microphone because:

  • It prevents websites from secretly accessing your microphone
  • You maintain full control over which sites can use your audio devices
  • You can revoke permission at any time through browser settings
  • It's required by web standards for user protection

We collect zero personal data. The tool operates entirely client-side, meaning all processing happens in your browser without sending any information to our servers. We don't track your usage, store your test results, or collect any personal information. This is a privacy-first tool designed to respect your data sovereignty.

If the test runs but shows no audio input, check these common issues:

  • Microphone muted: Check if your microphone is muted in system settings or on the device itself
  • Wrong input device: Use the device selector dropdown to choose the correct microphone
  • Low sensitivity: Increase microphone volume in your system's audio settings
  • Hardware issue: Test the microphone with another application to verify it's working
  • Driver problems: Update your audio drivers if using an external microphone
