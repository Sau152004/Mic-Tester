Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

This microphone test application is designed with privacy in mind. We want you to know exactly how your data is handled when you use our service.

What We Do

  • Process your microphone audio locally in your browser for real-time visualization
  • Display volume levels and frequency data to help you test your microphone
  • Provide a secure, client-side microphone testing experience

What We Don't Do

  • We never record your audio - Your microphone input is processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • We never transmit your audio - All audio processing happens locally in your browser
  • We never store your audio - No audio data is saved to our servers or your device
  • We don't track you - We don't use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts

Technical Details

Our microphone test uses the following browser technologies:

  • getUserMedia API - To access your microphone with your permission
  • Web Audio API - To analyze audio frequency and volume data in real-time
  • Canvas/DOM - To display visual representations of your audio

All processing happens entirely within your web browser. No audio data ever leaves your device.

Permissions

When you click "Start Microphone Test", your browser will ask for permission to access your microphone. This permission is:

  • Required only for the duration of the test
  • Can be revoked at any time through your browser settings
  • Used solely for microphone testing and visualization
  • Not used for any other purpose

Third-Party Services

This application uses minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts - For typography (Inter font family)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN - For styling components

These services do not have access to your microphone or audio data.

Data Security

Since no audio data is transmitted or stored, there are no data security concerns related to your microphone input. The application runs entirely in your browser using standard web technologies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please feel free to contact us.

Privacy Summary

Your audio is never recorded, transmitted, or stored. All processing happens locally in your browser for your privacy and security.

This microphone test runs entirely in your browser. No audio data is transmitted or stored.
