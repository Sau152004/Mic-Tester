Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data with zero collection and complete client-side processing.

Last updated: December 2024

Zero Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data or audio recordings.

Client-Side Processing

All audio analysis happens locally in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

Full User Control

You control all permissions and can revoke microphone access anytime.

Our Privacy Commitment

This privacy policy explains how the Microphone Test Tool handles your data and protects your privacy.

What We DON'T Collect

  • Audio recordings: We never record, store, or transmit any audio from your microphone
  • Personal information: No names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Usage analytics: No tracking, cookies, or behavioral data collection
  • Device fingerprinting: No unique device identification or tracking
  • IP addresses or location data: No server logs or location tracking

How Our Technology Works

Our microphone test tool operates using client-side web technologies:

1

Browser API Access

We request microphone access through your browser's Web Audio API

2

Real-time Analysis

Audio is processed in real-time using JavaScript in your browser

3

Local Processing

All analysis, visualization, and reports are generated locally

4

Immediate Disposal

Audio data is immediately discarded after analysis - nothing is saved

Browser Permissions & Your Control

When you use our tool, your browser will ask for microphone permission. Here's what you need to know:

Permission is required: Modern browsers require explicit user consent for microphone access
You're in control: You can deny, allow, or revoke permission at any time
Session-based: Permission is only active while you're using our tool
Easy to revoke: Use your browser's settings or address bar icon to manage permissions

Third-Party Services

Our tool uses minimal external dependencies:

Tailwind CSS: Loaded from CDN for styling (no data collection)
Google Fonts: Inter font loaded for consistent typography
jsPDF: Client-side PDF generation for certificates (no server interaction)

Important: These services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns. However, none of these services have access to your microphone or audio data.
